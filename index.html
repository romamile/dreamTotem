AR exploration for Dream Totem



Press "Start AR" below, and follow directions

1) Move your phone around, pointing at the ground, until a reticle appears

2) Touch the screen, when the reticle is visible, to place the sculpture

3) Move around the sculpture (and trigger at hover a change of color of face)

4) Touch when a face is in color, to make visible/invisible a face of the outer shell
